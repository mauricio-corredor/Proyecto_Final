About us

Despite the rise of digital music platforms such as iTunes or Spotify, there is a growing group of people who prefer to listen to music in traditional formats such as vinyl records. It is for this reason that Vinyland arises, an application that provides the amateur collector with the possibility of acquiring them new or used, either through purchase or exchange.

How it works

It is expected that through this system, visiting users and collectors will be able to find out about the available vinyls. For each one, the information of the musicians and songs and collectors who sell or exchange albums.

Join us!

Contribute to the album catalog, add tracks and your favorite artists. Share the albums you collect and the ones you are looking for. Connect with other collectors and comment on the discs available.

The best experience

Our collectors offer the best albums in the Classical, Folk, Rock and Salsa genres. There are already more than 1000 discs available for purchase, sale or exchange.
